Where I'm from

I was born in December 28th, in a city called Caxias do Sul (pictured above). It's located at the extreme south of Brazil and it has 500 thousand citizens. People usually think of sun and beaches when thinking of Brazil, but by town is really cold. It snows almost every year, and because of that, I was glad when I moved to Porto Alegre, my state capital. The state has the highest life expectancy in Brazil, and the crime rate is relatively low compared to the Brazilian national average.
